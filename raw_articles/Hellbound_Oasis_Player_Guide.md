# Hellbound on Oasis — player guide

**Hellbound** is a dangerous island tied to the **Steel Citadel**. On Oasis, its **stage** (you may also hear **level**) is a **server-wide** value: everyone shares the same Hellbound progress. When it goes up, the world usually announces it—watch those messages to know what tier the island is on.

This guide uses **Hellbound** and **stage** the way you’ll see them in game. Names of places and NPCs match what appears on your screen.

---

## How Hellbound gets stronger

**Fighting and doing content on the island** fills **trust** with Hellbound. When trust reaches the top of the current tier, the island often moves to the **next stage** on its own.

**Separately**, Oasis also **raises Hellbound by one stage on a fixed schedule**: **every other Friday at 6:00 PM server time** (so **once every two weeks**, not every week), with a broadcast when it happens. Even if trust progress is slow, the island can still **creep forward** on that timer—until it hits the highest stage the server allows.

If Hellbound is **dormant** (nothing meaningful unlocked yet), scheduled bumps **won’t** advance it; it has to be **active** at least at **stage 1** first.

**Example:** the automatic **stage 1 → 2** bump happened on **17 April 2026** (Friday, 6:00 PM server time). The **next** automatic bump after any given tick is **two weeks later** on the same weekday and time—watch **world announcements** to confirm each step.

## What opens at each stage

Stages stack: higher stages keep what lower ones unlocked, and add more.

| Stage | What you gain (in short) |
|--------|---------------------------|
| **1** | **Hellbound is live.** Outer areas, camps, and early island content are in play. Key figures like **Buron** and **Kief** are where newcomers expect them (near the harbor early on). |
| **2** | **Deeper northern / temple-style areas** get more dangerous spawns (e.g. **Remnant** activity in the **Ancient Temple** direction). |
| **3** | **Jude** offers the **Native Treasure → Ring of Wind Mastery** trade. **Keltas** and similar threats appear in their usual haunts. **Hude** starts to open up more. |
| **4** | **Derek** (*First Generation Seer*) appears. Defeating him at this stage **pushes Hellbound straight to stage 5**—a big shortcut. Some **low-stage-only** village events (like the starving natives) are still in their early form. |
| **5** | **Desert Quarry** is fully active (guards, slaves, quarry NPCs). **Leodas** and the **Dolmen** show up as intended. **Secret doors in the Refugee Camp** open so you can move through the sealed passages. **Buron** and **Kief** shift to the **village** side. **Solomon**’s situation reflects the stronger island. |
| **6** | **Hellinark** and the spawns around that **hellhound** content appear. Natives are treated as **past the “starving on arrival”** crisis (the island feels more stable). |
| **7** | A **major ground door** opens on the **eastern / wounded** approach (the **“injury earth”** barrier—deep demon country). **Chimera**-related threats appear. **Kief**’s **badges** and **life-force** trades matter a lot here. |
| **8** | **Outpost Captain** content is active near the **outpost / Iron Tower** approach. Beating the **Outpost Captain** while Hellbound is still **stage 8** **jumps the island to stage 9**. |
| **9** | The **big Iron Tower exterior gate** opens—path deeper toward the **Steel Citadel** tower complex. Extra **citadel-side** figures spawn that only appear from this stage upward. |
| **10** | **Kanaf** can send you into the **Urban Area** instance. **Monster Foundry** workers and foremen appear in the foundry areas. |
| **11** | **Jerian** can admit your party to the **Tower of Infinitum** (usual party, positioning, and item/quest checks still apply). This is the **top tier** for normal Hellbound progression. |

## Doors and regions

Think of **doors** as **hard gates**: until Hellbound is high enough, some passages stay shut.

- **Around stage 5** — **Hidden passages in the Refugee Camp** open (the **secret town doors**).
- **Around stage 7** — The **eastern wounded-ground gate** opens.
- **Around stage 9** — The **Iron Tower outer gate** opens toward the citadel heights.

On the map you’ll see names like **Hellbound**, **Refugee Camp**, **Desert Quarry**, **Phantasmal Ridge**, and **Beleth’s Magic Circle** as you explore; doors connect these regions as stages rise.

---

## Tips

- **Read world chat** when Hellbound bumps—**every other Friday at 6 PM server time** on Oasis (not weekly).
- **Trust** still matters for **organic** stage ups between announcements.
- **Boss shortcuts** matter: **Derek** (4→5) and **Outpost Captain** (8→9) can skip a full trust grind **if** you kill them at the right stage.
- **Instances and raids** (Urban Area, Tower of Infinitum, etc.) still need their **normal party, quest, and item** requirements—Hellbound stage only removes the **island tier** lock.
