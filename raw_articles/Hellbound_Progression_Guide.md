# Hellbound progression — Oasis player guide

This guide is for **Oasis**. It uses **names you see in the game** (NPCs, items, places). You do **not** need a quest to **travel to Hellbound**; the island is open from launch in its starting state (there is no “dead zone” phase to unlock first).

---

## What Hellbound is

**Hellbound** is a hostile island region. The **Steel Citadel** is the large demon fortress complex on it. As players hunt and complete events on the island, **Hellbound’s level** rises. When it goes up, the server announces it to everyone. Higher levels open new spawns, doors, services, and instances.

You will also see **reputation with Hellbound** (trust) move up or down from kills and certain choices. When trust fills the current tier, Hellbound usually advances to the **next level** automatically. A few special kills force an early jump at specific levels (see below).

---

## Core ideas (plain language)

- **Hellbound level** — global progress for the whole server; watch world announcements.
- **Trust** — the island’s “mood” toward you; farming certain monsters adds it; some NPC trades add or subtract it.
- **Party leader** — several steps require a **full party**, and only the **party leader** may talk to the NPC or use the device, with everyone **close by**.

## Progression by Hellbound level (high level)

- **Levels 1–2** — Outer camps and basics; **Junior Watchman** and **Junior Summoner** kills help early trust while levels are low.
- **Level 3** — **Jude** offers the Native Treasure trade; **Hude** starts opening up.
- **Level 4** — Special beat: defeating **Derek** (*First Generation Seer*) pushes Hellbound straight to **level 5** (this bypasses the normal “fill the bar” step for that tier).
- **Level 5** — **Quarry** behavior and **Solomon** lines; more of the island feels “alive.”
- **Level 6+** — Natives no longer auto-starve on spawn; **Kief** and later mechanics matter more.
- **Level 7** — **Chimera** kills add trust; **Kief** deals scale with badges and life-force items.
- **Level 8** — Defeating **Outpost Captain** while Hellbound is still **8** advances the island to **level 9**.
- **Level 9** — **Native Slave** badge turn-in is active in the usual way.
- **Level 10** — **Kanaf** can start **Urban Area**; deeper citadel doors and enemies align with this tier; **Monster Foundry** logic expects **10+**.
- **Level 11** — **Jerian** can admit your party to the **Tower of Infinitum** shaft (see requirements below).

Exact monster lists change with spawns; when in doubt, follow announcements and which NPCs appear.

## Urban Area (with Kanaf)

**Requirement:** Hellbound **level 10** or higher.

**What you do there**

1. Enter the **Urban Area** instance through **Kanaf**.
2. Defeat the **Steel Citadel Keymaster** — on Oasis, **Key of the Evil Eye** is a **reliable** drop from this foe (bring a proper party/loot rules as usual).
3. Free or interact with **Native Prisoner** NPCs; **Amaskari** (*Torture Expert*) drives the script — killing **Amaskari** marks the instance state.
4. **Sculpture / device:** needs a **party of at least two**. The **party leader** uses **Key of the Evil Eye** near it (the key is **consumed**). Stay in range; after several minutes the instance **ejects** the group.

## Entering the Steel Citadel proper

- **Normal way:** advance Hellbound and fight through the outer areas until you reach the fortress.
- **Shortcut:** **Deltuva** teleports you inward **only if** **Matras’ Curiosity** is **completed** on that character (party auto-follow features still require every pulled member to have finished it).

---

## Labyrinth and passageway (keys and blood)

Work toward **Passageway Captain**, **Passageway Mystic**, **Labyrinth Seer**, **Labyrinth Watchman**, **Body Destroyer**, and **Soul Dominator** as you push deeper.

**On Oasis**, these gate items drop **deterministically** from their named holders (no frustrating rare roll):

| Item | Dropped by |
|------|------------|
| **Gate Key: Blood** | **Labyrinth Seer** |
| **Gate Key: Destruction** | **Passageway Captain** |
| **Gate Key: Darkness** | **Body Destroyer**, **Soul Dominator**, **Labyrinth Watchman** |
| **Fiery Demon Blood** | **Passageway Captain**, **Passageway Mystic** |

Use each **Gate Key** on the matching **demon gate** (use the item at the door). **Use Fiery Demon Blood** to receive the buff needed for **Jerian**’s check (or finish **Target of Opportunity** instead — see quests).

**Guzen** — defeating **Guzen** opens further passage; **Kendal** (*Wandering Ghost*) appears afterward. **Body Destroyer** uses a dangerous line attack; spread out and avoid feeding **Death Word**.

## Tower of Infinitum (Jerian)

**Requirements**

- Hellbound **level 11** or higher.
- A **party** whose **leader** speaks to **Jerian**.
- **Every** member near **Jerian** must have **either** the buff from using **Fiery Demon Blood** **or** a **completed** **Target of Opportunity** quest.

**Inside:** floor teleports need **at least two** party members, the **leader** initiating, everyone **nearby** and on the **same height** (floor) as the device. Use **Up** / **Down** to climb the shaft toward the upper citadel.

---

## Upper citadel content

- **Demon Prince** and **Ranku** — raid encounters tied to **Matras’ Curiosity** blueprint items and rough ores.
- **Tully’s Workshop** — multi-floor instance chain.
- **Monster Foundry** — tied to **Jude’s Request** for evil weapon materials.
- **Beleth** — end boss line; entry is through the **Teleport Cube** when the epic is available. Follow **party / command channel** requirements shown in game at that time.

## Troubleshooting

- **Kanaf will not start Urban Area** — Hellbound is still **below 10**; keep clearing island content until the level rises.
- **Jerian refuses the party** — Hellbound **below 11**, someone not in range, you are not **leader**, or a member lacks **Fiery Demon Blood** buff **and** has not **finished** **Target of Opportunity**.
- **Sculpture in Urban Area does nothing** — party smaller than two, members too far, or no **Key of the Evil Eye** on the leader.
- **Deltuva will not teleport** — **Matras’ Curiosity** not **completed** on that character.
- **Leodas closed again** — **Hellbound Traitor** doors are **timed**; they close after a while and must be paid for again with marks.
